The .m/.mat-files contain the functions that compute the Jacobians for
the different equations of motion. 

"don": simplified, without inertial coupling
"ama": with inertial coupling
"nd": non-dimensionalized
"jin": Jin's approach for the antenna with nonlinear strain-displacement
"lin": Jin's approach for the antenna with linear strain-displacement

The other naming-elements refer to the parameters of the model.
